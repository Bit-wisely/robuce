01 / CORE MISSION

Fostering Robotics & Embedded Engineering

Robotics Club UCE is the premiere collegiate community dedicated to exploring, designing, and fabricating next-generation robotic systems. We host weekly lab reviews, support technical student research, and compete in nationwide autonomous platform events.

Hardware Systems

Design custom printed circuit boards (PCBs), prototype high-frequency sensory interfaces, and deploy low-latency microcontrollers.

Autonomous Control

Implement real-time sensor fusion filter matrices, Robot Operating System (ROS) telemetry node configurations, and neural perception.

Mechanical Modeling

Create advanced 3D CAD schematics, perform stress tests under simulation engines, and fabricate components with 3D print units.
